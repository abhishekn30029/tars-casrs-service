About us

Who are we?

Tars Cars is India's only exclusive chain of Auto Collision Repair Centers. We have fully-owned completely transparent chain of car bodyshops, aimed at providing best in class service quality and customer satifaction. We are quickly growing throughout India.

OUR MISSION

WE OFFER QUALITY & FAST SERVICES

We believe that repairing a car is more than just fixing the technical issues - it is an art, to make sure that beautiful piece of metal gets the love and attention it needs.

    How we do it?

  1. When you entrust your vehicle to us, we’ll keep you informed every step of the way.
  2. Once we fully assess the damage, we’ll provide an expected completion date. Then we’ll do everything in our power to meet or beat that date.
  3. If there’s an unforeseen delay, we’ll inform you right away by phone, text or email – whatever works best for you.

You can also count on Tars Cars for convenience. We make it easy to file an insurance claim because we have relationships with nearly every Indian insurance provider. With free pickup and drop across the length and breadth of the cities, we are virtually next door to you.
